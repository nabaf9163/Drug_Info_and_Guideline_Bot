Privacy Policy for MedInfo Bot

Effective Date: February 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MedInfo Bot. We provide drug information and treatment guidelines via WhatsApp and Telegram messaging platforms. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information is handled responsibly.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our bot.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

A. Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Your WhatsApp or Telegram phone number.
  • Profile Name: The display name associated with your messaging account.
  • User ID: A unique identifier provided by the messaging platform (e.g., WhatsApp ID, Telegram Chat ID).

B. Interaction Data

  • Messages: The text queries you send to the bot (e.g., "What is metformin?").
  • Usage Data: Current country/region selection, timestamps of interactions, and command usage.
  • Session Context: Temporary conversation history maintained during your active session to provide context-aware responses.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To process your queries and generate medical information responses.
  • Contextual Understanding: To maintain conversation continuity (e.g., remembering your selected country or previous question).
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the accuracy of bot responses.
  • Communication: To send you requested information (e.g., drug guidelines).

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. However, in order to provide our service, we share data with the following trusted third-party providers:

  • Google Cloud Platform (Firebase): We use Google Cloud services to host the bot application and store session data. Data is processed and stored securely on Google servers.
  • Google Gemini API: Your text queries are sent to Google's Gemini AI service to generate natural language responses. This data is processed according to Google's AI data policies.
  • Meta (WhatsApp) & Telegram: As messaging platforms, they transmit your messages to us. Their use of your data is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Retention

  • Session Data: Active session data (including recent conversation history) is stored temporarily to facilitate ongoing chats. Sessions expire automatically after a period of inactivity (e.g., 30 minutes).
  • Logs: Server logs containing interaction metadata may be retained for debugging and security purposes for a limited time.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Correction: You can update your preferences (e.g., country selection) using bot commands like /country.
  • Deletion: You can reset your current session and clear immediate context by typing /cancel.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop using the service at any time by simply ceasing interaction or blocking the bot contact.

7. Medical Disclaimer

MedInfo Bot is an information tool only and does not provide medical advice.

  • The content provided is for informational purposes and guidelines.
  • Users should verify information with official sources or healthcare professionals.
  • We rely on third-party AI models which may occasionally produce inaccuracies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
