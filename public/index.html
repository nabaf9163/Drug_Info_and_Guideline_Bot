Counseling for Griseofulvin
💊 WHAT IT IS FOR
• Griseofulvin treats fungal infections of skin, hair, and nails (ringworm, athlete's foot).

💊 HOW TO TAKE
• Take with a fatty meal (milk, ice cream) to help absorption.
• Complete the full course even if you feel better.

⚠️ SIDE EFFECTS
• Headache (common at start)
• Sun sensitivity (use sunscreen)
• Dizziness (be careful driving)

🚨 SEEK HELP IF
• Severe rash or blistering
• Yellowing of skin/eyes
• Unusual bleeding

What is metformin used for?
💊 INDICATION
• Type 2 Diabetes Mellitus (first-line)
• PCOS (off-label)

💉 DOSE
• Adult: Start 500 mg BID, max 2550 mg/day
• Pediatric (≥10y): 500 mg BID, max 2000 mg/day

⚠️ MONITOR
• eGFR at baseline + annually
• HbA1c every 3–6 months
